CARD GAME

INSTRUCTION

Follow the following instructions to play the game

  • Enter the number of cards to be dealt to each player in the input form.
  • Click on the DEAL button to deal cards to each player
  • UPPER HAND :- stacks of cards at the top.
    LOWER HAND:- stacks of cards at he bottom.
  • To add more cards to either of the hands, click on the bottom indicating the hands first then click on the pile of cards on the left (MARKET).
